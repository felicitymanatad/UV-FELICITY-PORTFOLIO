Digital Marketing Campaign

Led a team project to develop and execute a digital marketing campaign for a local nonprofit organization. Utilized Google Ads and social media advertising platforms to increase brand awareness and drive traffic to the organization's website.

Live Link

Project Overview

involved leading a team to create and implement a comprehensive digital marketing strategy for a local nonprofit organization. The primary goal was to enhance brand visibility, engage the target audience, and channel traffic to the organization's website.

Target Audience: The campaign would have been tailored to reach the target audience most likely to be interested in the new product line. This could include demographic targeting (age, gender, location, interests) and psychographic targeting (lifestyle, values, behavior).


Marketing Strategy Development: Develop a comprehensive marketing strategy that includes the following elements:
1. Banner Ads on Websites: Created visually appealing banner ads to be displayed on relevant websites frequented by the target audience. These ads may have included compelling visuals, concise messaging, and a clear call-to-action (CTA) to drive traffic to the organization's website or specific landing pages.
2. Retargeting Campaigns: Implemented retargeting campaigns using Google Ads and social media platforms to reconnect with users who had previously visited the organization's website. This strategy aimed to remind them of the organization's mission, events, or donation opportunities, encouraging them to take further action.
3. Contextual and Placement Targeting: Utilized contextual targeting to ensure ads appeared on websites related to nonprofit causes, community events, or local news. Additionally, may have used placement targeting to select specific websites or platforms aligned with the target audience's interests and demographics.
4. Dynamic Ads for Personalization: Leveraged dynamic ads to personalize content based on user behavior or interests. For example, showing different ad content to users who had previously engaged with specific campaigns or donation pages, tailoring the messaging to their interests and engagement level.
5. Native Advertising on Social Media: Developed native ads on social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, or Twitter to seamlessly blend with users' feeds. These ads may have included stories, sponsored posts, or promoted content to engage users authentically and encourage social sharing or interaction.
6. Video Ads for Engagement: Created video ads to capture attention and convey the organization's story, impact, or upcoming events. Video ads may have been used on platforms like YouTube, Facebook, or Instagram, leveraging the power of visuals and storytelling to drive engagement and emotional connection.
